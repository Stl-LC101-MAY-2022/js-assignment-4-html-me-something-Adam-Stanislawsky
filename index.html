How to make espresso

or, one weird trick to spend all your disposable income

Let's make a shot of espresso! There are many parameters to consider when making espresso, however the basic concept is this: We are using heat and pressure to extract delicious juice from a small puck of finely ground coffee.

Espresso is a beverage, but the process of creating it often becomes a hobby for many. It is very easy to fall down the rabbit hole of perpetually acuiring new gear in the hopes of marginally improving the quality of your espresso.

Tools you will need:

  • espresso machine
  • espresso grinder
  • tamper
  • scale
  • patience (optional)

Steps to make espresso:

  1. weigh ~18 grams of coffee beans → (why weigh?)
  2. grind finely → (watch this for more detail)
  3. distribute ground coffee evenly in filter basket→ (watch this for more detail)
  4. pull shot
  5. taste, adjust parameters for your next shot!→ (troubleshooting guide)
